Our Journey

Founded in 2008, Pingshan Weiying began with a vision to supply premium garden and landscape materials to projects that demand uncompromising quality. Over the past 15+ years we have grown from a small trading firm into a global partner serving 135+ countries.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable, high-performance materials that empower our customers to build greener, safer and more inspiring environments.

Our Vision

To be the world’s most trusted source for innovative garden and landscape solutions, leading the transition to environmentally-responsible construction materials.
